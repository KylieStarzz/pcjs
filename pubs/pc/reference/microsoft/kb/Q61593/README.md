	The following is the signal configuration of the 9-pin Hosiden socket
	on the bus adapter of the Microsoft Mouse:
	
	   Pin          Signal
	   ---        ------
	
	   1          +5v
	   2           XA
	   3           XB
	   4           YA
	   5           YB
	   6           SW1
	   7           SW2
	   8           SW3
	   9           Signal ground
	   E           Shield ground
